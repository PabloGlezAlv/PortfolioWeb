Space Scape (2024)

  • Tech Stack: Unreal 5, Blueprints
  • Github URL: Project Link
  • Demo Video: Video Link
A small horror game where the player has to explore the area, finding objects to stun the enemy until they find the key and can escape. The enemy use a behaviour tree moves randomly around the map until it sees the player and chases them. The game is made with free assets.
